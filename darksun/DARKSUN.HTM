As you open the old wooden door, you hear an old, low voice whispering to you:
How nice of you to come all the way from the surface to visit me, in my modest home. You are my guests. Behind a large wooden desk sits an ancient undead creature, the size of a human. It rizes from behind the desk, and speaks to you:"I am Morgreth, the first law-keeper of Lower Nibenay. Welcome."

The Dark Sun campaign setting is my favourite one from TSR. I've run several campaigns on Athas, and have written quite some material about the world and its inhabitants myself. You will find the best of it here.

Athas is a desert world. Centuries of defiling spells used by evil sorcerors and dragons has turned this once forest-covered world into wasteland. The people of Athas live in city states that used to be ruled by powerful sorceror kings. The adventures and novels written for Dark Sun have seen the death of some of these half-dragons.
The Dark Sun product line is currently on a halt, though the Dark Sun Revised Boxed Set will be re-released somewhere in 1999. Meanwhile, the Dark Sun online community keeps the dark sun setting alive. There are dozens of dark sun pages on the net, far more than there are for any other world.

I have previously maintained two dark sun pages: first the Wanderer's Home, later the Crypt. Now, the material from these sites can be found here, together with the keeper of that material, known only as Morgreth.
