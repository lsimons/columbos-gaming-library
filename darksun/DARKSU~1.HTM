Morgreth

So, you want to know who I am, huh? Well sit down and relax, for this will take some time...

'Once upon a time, in the ancient past, even before there was magic, before there was psionics, there was water. The whole surface of Athas was covered with water. In a small area, which is know called "The Tyr Region", there was a great civilization of halflings.

When this civilization came to an end, the seas dissapeared, to make place for Green Forest. Many new races were born, and these races built great cities. On the ruins of ancient Nibenay, a new city was founded. The law-enforcers of this city were undead, created by the combined efforts of priests and psionicists. These guardians were called Meorty. After the Magic and The Cleansing Wars came, the land withered to desert, and the great cities became ruins. The undead, still exist however, to enforce the ancient laws, bound by their undead curse. I, Morgreth, am the law-enforcer of Nibenay.

Human male F17; AC 1 (chain mail=4); MV 12; hp 112; THAC0 4; #AT 1; Dmg 1d8+3 (long sword+3); MR 90%; SZ M; ML Fearless (20); AL LE; S 17, D 14, C 18, I 12, W 14, C 11;
Special Undead Powers: Cause Paralysis, Control Undead, Magical Resistance;
Special Undead Weaknesses: Bound to an Area; Sunlight Vulnerability.
