'You people there on your planet Terra explain your universe, your plane by a set of rules you call "physical laws", or something. Other planes, other planets have their own ways of explaining their universe.
'All these different worlds, with all their different "laws" can be explained using what is known as the adnd game system. Yet every planet needs it own modifications for this system to work. And the system is far from perfect. So we change the laws, we change the system. And then there's a new planet, and there are new laws. And so the cycle goes on.'

ADND. A great game. Unlimited possibilities. These unlimited possibilities go even furhter as you write your own material. Many have done this, to a more or less successful result. In this section you will find custom material by me, and very good material by a few others.
