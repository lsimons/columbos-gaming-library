Rule Modifications

'Many people just rely on the rules given by the Creators, the lords of TSR. But there are many more rules in this universe, which are often even better than the rules of the creators. Here I give you the best.'

Mass Combat System
These rules of my own creation give you a complete, fast, easy, smooth and realistic system for playing out combats between large armies. Covers all tactics and troop types in a few pages.
Rating:****
'my creation, 2nd in the Insane DM Houserules series

Adventurer Rules
Shortly said, these easy rules allow you to create an adventurer with ANY combination of abilities you want. Using character points, these rules give you more options than the class creation rules in the DMG.
Rating:*****
'my creation, 1st in the Insane DM Houserules series

Time Unit Combat System (RTF)
These rules do away with the classical round system. Instead, combat just flows from second to second. Fast and fluent rules. I wonder why the Creators never thought of this.
Rating:***
