Players' Guide to Drakin

The World of Drakin comes with a seperate book especially for the players. It presents the new races, a new character generation system, and an introduction to Drakin.

Meet klingons, powerful humanoids with bulky forheads and a warrior culture. Meet green dwarves, which live in burrows deep in the Great Primeval forest. Meet Dorians, mysterious desert nomads that command dangerous psionics. And of course, all the standard races are present as well.

Find out what powers a Dragon Warrior possesses, and learn of the Elven Ninja's. The Player Guide has been updated and now futures the Adventurer Rule System, allowing any combination of abilities.
