Barons and Sages Expansion

The Barons and Sages book is invaluable for any DM that wants to run a campaign with characters levels 9 and up. It gives new rules and advice on various topics. In this revised tome, I have included a system for resolving wars between large numbers of troops.

There is information on the nobility of the Known World, and there is more. Learn about the awesome power of the dragons of drakin, and High Sorcery, the most powerful magic ever seen. Learn of new rules for followers, of how to create an adventure for high-level characters, and of the Faith and the Church.
