DM's Guide to Drakin

The singlemost important book to all Dungeon Masters who want to master Drakin is the DM's Guide to the Dragon Kings Campaign. It introduces you to the world of Drakin, and draws you into its atmosphere.

The DM's Guide gives you a detailed history. Learn about Nok, the great psionicist who almost destroyed civilisation. Learn about Drako and Rabak, the first and most powerful of all dragons, and of their struggle which killed both.

Discover the kingdoms of the known world, and meet their powerful Dragon Kings. Discover the Misty Plains, and their connection to the gothic horror of Ravenloft. Learn of The Church, the largest power in the Known World. Explore the Known World now!
