Click here for an introduction to the World of Drakin.

These internet pages are the guide to the Dragon Kings© AD&D© campaign setting. The world is as detailed as an official TSR© setting, with history, culture, adventures, geography, and on.

The World of Drakin is a world ruled by powerful dragons/sorcerors, a world with nobles, a powerful Faith, and deadly klingon armies. The world is playable all the way from level 1 to 30, and I give you new rules for mass combat, castles, followers, and on, and on, and on.

All of this is free, easily downloadable from this site. All you'll have to do is unzip it and print it out. All files should be viewable by everyone: they are either RTF, PCX or WMF (depending on the filetype.)

The only thing I ask from you is that you'll send any ideas or material you have to improve the world of Drakin to me, via E-mail.
