Columbo's Journals: DragonMountain

No city in the Known World can come close to DragonMountain when you look at wealth, adventure, size, or just about anything else.

DragonMountain has many names, but the most important one is that of "The Free City". DragonMountain has long ago lost its dragon, and has never been conquered by one again. Today, it is ruled by the Seven Houses: powerful noble merchant imperiums. Who rules tomorrow can be discovered through the quest series...

This is the first book from the pen of the renowded doctor Columbo, the most famous explorer of the Known World. I've corrected some of his errors, though.

Do you use DragonMountain? If so, I would love it if you could send me any and all buildings, NPCs, adventures, and more that is related to the city. That way, DragonMountain can grow larger for the better of all of us!
