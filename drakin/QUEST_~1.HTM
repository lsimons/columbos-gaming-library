The Quest Series Adventures
The Plotline (14 kb). This RTF file describes the story of the Quest Series.

This series of linked adventures takes your players from level 1 to level 30. At first the events seem totally unrelated, but soon a monstruos conspiracy unfolds before you and the players.

I planned to upload each of these adventures for the second time, with my newest gaming group. However, since they screwed up the very first adventure (The Lost Monks), I don't think much of it will be up soon. Instead, I'm running the players through the Grand Conjunction adventures (free download at the TSR site) in Ravenloft.
The Magic Maniac (20 kb). This adventure is an introduction to the World of Drakin and the kickstart for the Quest Series. Easy to run for anyone.
The Lost Monks (28 kb). This adventure starts where the Magic Maniac ended: the village of Tork. The players must find the lost monks and bring them back alive. Do you smell garlic too?
