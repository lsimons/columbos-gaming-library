Rule Modifications

'Y

ou people there on your planet Terra explain your universe, your plane by a set of rules you call "physical laws", or something. Other planes, other planets have their own ways of explaining their universe.
All these different worlds, with all their different "laws", can be explained using what is known as the adnd game system. Yet every planet needs it own modifications for this system to work. And the system is far from perfect. So we change the laws, we change the system. And then there's a new planet, and there are new laws. And so the cycle goes on.'
A

DND is a great game system with lots and lots of options and rules. Yet, almost nobody finds the rules completely to his satisfaction. Neither do I. In the years I have played the game, I have tried many different adaptions to the rules. Of course, I have located and corrected many flaws in the system. But these I will not share with you.
What I present here are my own, new rules. They all change something, something I thought had to be changed. Maybe you think different, maybe not. Either way, enjoy the reading.

Mass Combat System By Leo Simons
These rules of my own creation give you a complete, fast, easy, smooth and realistic system for playing out combats between large armies. Covers all tactics and troop types in a few pages.

Adventurer Rules By Leo Simons
Shortly said, these easy rules allow you to create an adventurer with ANY combination of abilities you want. Using character points, these rules give you more options than the class creation rules in the DMG.

Time Unit Combat System By Nils-Børge Malkenes and others
These rules do away with the classical round system. Instead, combat just flows from second to second. Fast and fluent rules. I wonder why the TSR designers never thought of this.
