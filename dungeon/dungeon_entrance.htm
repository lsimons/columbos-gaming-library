Snyad Surprise By Philip B. Copper III
This nasty little adventure can be used as a sidetrek in every place of every dungeon. The PCs get shrunken to a few inches high. Then, they get to deal with some very nice roleplaying situations, as well as with a giant rat...very, very good.

SECTION: The Cavern of Nirin By Luka Rejec
This section of the dungeon is a large cave, with several cities in it. Each of these is controlled by a particular race. In the middle of the cave is a large and dangerous maze, with an exit to the dungeon in the center. Rejec is still working on this one...
