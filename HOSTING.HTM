Have some great adnd material?

That's easy now!

I will host any and all material relating to the adnd hobby, or to a specific campaign setting. There are no requirments, no obligations, there's just a nice chap (that's me)who wants to bring the best material to everyone.

You can have this stuff in one of my sections, or you can make your own html page, or you can let me create a new section for your file. It doesn't matter!

The only thing that might scare you off is that I will rate all material on a scale from one to five. That means that if your material is really bad, it recieves a one-star rating. (but if your stuff is bad, why share it with the 'net community anyway?)

The only drawback with this is that your URL isn't that easy, and that you will have to host a small advertisement at the top of your page.
